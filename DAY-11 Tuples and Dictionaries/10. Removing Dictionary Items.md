### 4. Removing Items (`pop()`, `del`, `clear()`)
**Input:**
```python
thisdict = {"brand": "Ford", "model": "Mustang", "year": 1964}

# Remove specific item
thisdict.pop("model")
print(thisdict)

# Delete key entirely
del thisdict["year"]
print(thisdict)

# Clear dictionary
thisdict.clear()
print(thisdict)
```
**Output:**
```text
{'brand': 'Ford', 'year': 1964}
{'brand': 'Ford'}
{}
```
