### 3. Adding and Updating Items
**Input:**
```python
thisdict = {"brand": "Ford", "model": "Mustang"}

# Add new key
thisdict["color"] = "red"
print(thisdict)

# Update existing key
thisdict.update({"year": 2020})
print(thisdict)
```
**Output:**
```text
{'brand': 'Ford', 'model': 'Mustang', 'color': 'red'}
{'brand': 'Ford', 'model': 'Mustang', 'color': 'red', 'year': 2020}
```
