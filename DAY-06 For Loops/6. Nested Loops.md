## 6. Nested Loops
A nested loop is a loop inside a loop. The "inner loop" will be executed one time for each iteration of the "outer loop".
**Input:**
```python
adj = ["red", "big"]
fruits = ["apple", "cherry"]

for a in adj:
    for f in fruits:
        print(a, f)
```
**Output:**
```text
red apple
red cherry
big apple
big cherry
```
