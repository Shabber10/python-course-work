## 5. Escape Characters
To insert characters that are illegal in a string, use an escape character `\`.
- `\'` Single Quote
- `\\` Backslash
- `\n` New Line
- `\t` Tab

**Input:**
```python
txt = "We are the so-called \"Vikings\" from the north.\nNew Line."
print(txt)
```
**Output:**
```text
We are the so-called "Vikings" from the north.
New Line.
```
