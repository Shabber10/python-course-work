## 1. Modifying Strings (Case and Trimming)

### `upper()` & `lower()`
**Input:**
```python
a = "Hello, World!"
print(a.upper())
print(a.lower())
```
**Output:**
```text
HELLO, WORLD!
hello, world!
```

### `strip()`
Removes whitespace from the beginning and the end.
**Input:**
```python
a = " Hello, World! "
print(a.strip())
```
**Output:**
```text
Hello, World!
```
